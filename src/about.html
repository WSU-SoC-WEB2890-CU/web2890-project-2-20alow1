About

The Business

The Quilting Bee specializes in finishing your quilt tops! We offer longarm machine quilting services, quilt binding, and carry products you may need to finish your quilt. We carry batting, backing fabrics and blender fabrics. We also carry finished quilts for those who may not want to make their own quilts.

The Quilting Bee is also located on the basement level of the Winward Building at 110 E Main Street, Wellsville, UT 84339

The Owner

Aleena Winward is the owner of The Quilting Bee in Wellsville. She started a machine quilting business in her home in 2011 using her Gammill Vision 30-12 for hand-driven machine quilting. She is a certified machine quilter with training from Village Dry Goods and Rocky Mountain Electric Quilters. She has enjoyed learning in classes she's taken from many wonderful teachers at local quilt shops and local quilt guilds in Cache Valley. She enjoys free motion quilting overall patterns best.

The Origins

Quilting is part of Aleena's heritage. Her grandma made hundreds of quilts by hand for her family. Grandma taught Aleena about quilting and helped her make her first t-shirt quilt. When Aleena got her longarm quilting machine, she felt as though she had come home and knew grandma must be smiling in Heaven. With the help of her quilting machine, Aleena can quilt more quilts than even grandma had, with sturdier stitches. She owes her love of quilting to grandma!

The Winward Building

Aleena and her husband Sam started The Winward Building in 2018. The building also houses other family businesses:

  • Sam operates his Financial Services Office on the main level.
  • Also on the main level, the Winward kids and other local teenagers help run The Wellsville Cone, a small ice cream shop where locals can come and enjoy famous Aggie Ice Cream and Creamies.
  • The Legacy Loft Reception Center is on the second level, where people can rent space to hold wedding receptions, family parties, business meetings, recitals, or performances.
