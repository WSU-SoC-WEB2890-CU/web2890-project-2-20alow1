Quilting Services & Prices

These are all the quilting services Aleena offers, but feel free to pick and choose the ones you want.

Freehand Overall Quilting (edge to edge)

$.0175 ‐ $.02 per square inch, with a minimum of $35.

The basic meandering stitch is $.0175 per square inch, and all other stitches are $.02 per square inch.

Our goal is to reach a fair price for you and us. We time the quilting to determine the overall price. Keep in mind that your quilt top being precise and well prepared can affect the price. If it takes longer than normal, we may charge extra.

Custom Quilting

$35/hour, with a minimum of $45.

Binding

$0.15/inch ‐ Machine bind 1 side only.

$0.20/inch ‐ Machine bind both sides.

$0.30/inch ‐ Machine bind 1 side and hand bind the 2nd side.

The prices above include us making the binding, but you must give us the fabric to make the binding.

Quilt Top Piecing or Fabric Prep

$35/hour.

A nice friend or relative of yours will be a better deal for you. However, if you would like us to piece for you, please contact us directly to discuss your project.

Rush Jobs (Need it in less than 2 weeks)

$25 additional
